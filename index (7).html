About Me

I am a graduate geography researcher and GIS specialist focused on climate displacement, hazard vulnerability, environmental justice, and spatial decision-making. My work combines GIS analysis, hazard mapping, LiDAR processing, and spatial statistics to better understand how climate risk and social vulnerability shape unequal outcomes for communities.

I am especially interested in environmental justice GIS, emergency response, hazard mitigation, climate adaptation, and resilience planning.
